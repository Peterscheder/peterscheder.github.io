The terrible pirates from Barbados

Back in the day, when the really old trees that are standing today were really young. When democracy was considered something very retro. Back then there was a pirate ship cruising the caribbean. On this Ship lived a few characters which I want to introduce.

The Captain

He was a big old jerk, behind his back the crew called him jerkoff.

The Helmsman

He held the wheel all day long. Most of the time he was asleep.

The Deckhand

He had to do all the work. Of this situation he had gotten quite tired. So wrecked his brains all say long about how to desert the ship.

The Cook

He was a former Ballet Dancer, they had captured him during a raid. He knew as much about cooking as President Zuma about Aids prevention.

The Shipmonkey

The Monkey lived high up in the mast. He only came down to eat or play tricks on the foul smelling captain.
